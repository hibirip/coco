Debug Coin Check

Please check the browser console at http://localhost:5175/prices

Look for these debug messages:

  • 🔍 PriceContext ALL_SYMBOLS 개수: (should be 100)
  • 🔍 getBatchTickerData 호출 전 - ALL_SYMBOLS 개수: (should be 100)
  • 🔍 getBatchTickerData - 요청된 심볼 개수: (should be 100)
  • 🔍 fetchAllBitgetTickersData - 받은 전체 티커 개수: (actual tickers from Bitget)
  • 🔍 getBatchTickerData - 성공한 심볼 개수: (how many matched)
  • 🔍 getBatchTickerData - 찾지 못한 심볼: (which symbols are missing)
  • 🔍 PricesPage - ALL_SYMBOLS 개수: (should be 100)
  • 🔍 PricesPage - prices 개수: (actual loaded prices)
  • 🔍 PricesPage - 필터링 후 코인 개수: (final display count)
